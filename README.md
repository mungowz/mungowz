# Alfredo Mungari
### Data Scientist · Machine Learning · Software Engineer

Based in Naples, Italy. Currently completing an MSc in Applied Computer Science (Machine Learning & Big Data) at the University of Naples "Parthenope" (Expected: April 2026), after graduating BSc with **110/110 Summa Cum Laude with Special Mention**.

---

## Current Work & Research

- **Master's Thesis (CI&SS Lab):** Researching and implementing conditional generative models (*Diffusion Models*) applied to tabular data generation and synthetic data augmentation.
- **Explainable AI (XAI):** Applying Feature Importance analysis and XAI techniques to ensure interpretability and transparency of complex predictive models.
- **Data Mining:** Extracting association rules via *Market Basket Analysis* to identify recurring patterns in large-scale datasets.

- Research Intern at the **National Research Council (CNR)**, where I developed [AUDO4RIAS](https://github.com/mungowz/AUDO4RIAS) — a tool to automate the *molecular docking* process using bioinformatics libraries.
- **Programming Tutor** (2023–2025) at the University of Naples "Parthenope", supporting 30+ students per semester on C/C++ algorithms and data structures.
